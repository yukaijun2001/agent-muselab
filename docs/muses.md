# The nine Muses

> [简体中文](muses_zh.md)

muselab takes its name from the **nine Muses** of Greek mythology — the
goddesses who inspire art and learning. **Muse** is the AI persona within;
**muselab** is the workspace it inhabits.

On first visit, a Muse is picked at random; she stays put until you
click the mascot in the chat header to cycle. The favicon and browser
tab update accordingly.

| Muse | Domain | Geometric form |
|---|---|---|
| Calliope | Epic poetry | Hexagon |
| Clio | History | Stacked horizontal bars (scroll layers) |
| Erato | Love poetry | Vesica piscis (lens) |
| Euterpe | Music | Sine wave |
| Melpomene | Tragedy | Crescent |
| Polyhymnia | Sacred hymns | Halo ring |
| Terpsichore | Dance | Three dots (triangular) |
| Thalia | Comedy | Spark |
| Urania | Astronomy | Orbit |
