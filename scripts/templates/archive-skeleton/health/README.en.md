# health/

Body-related: checkup reports, medications, supplements, training logs,
long-term conditions.

## What goes here

- Checkup report PDFs (suggested: `YYYY-MM-checkup-clinic.pdf`)
- Current medications / supplements (dose, brand, why)
- Exercise / training logs
- Sleep / heart rate / steps exports
- Doctor visit records, allergies, chronic conditions
- Health of family members — sub-folders per person

## Muse's constraints here

- No diagnosis, no prescriptions; defers to a real doctor on important decisions
- Cites mainstream guidelines (AHA / ADA / USPSTF / equivalent national bodies),
  not popular media
- When giving numbers: include units, dose, time window, side effects,
  contraindications
- No MLM supplements; no exaggerated supplement claims
- Preserves numbers as written — Muse will quote specific values
- Watches units (mmol/L vs mg/dL, ng/mL vs nmol/L)

## Notes

- Redact medical record numbers / national ID before saving
- For mental health / sexual health / psychiatric medication: consider a
  separately-encrypted file
