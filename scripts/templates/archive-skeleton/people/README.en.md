# people/

Notes about people in this archive owner's life — partner, parents, kids,
housemates, long-term friends, mentors, pets.

## What goes here

- A short note per important person (age / health / life stage / relationship dynamic)
- Family decisions (caregiving arrangements / education plans / household split)
- Partner / marriage notes (shared goals / recent events)
- Health checklist, policies, key dates for parents / elders
- Kids' growth records (if applicable)
- Important dates (birthdays / anniversaries / surgery dates / move dates)

## Muse's constraints here

- Connects across dimensions on big decisions (e.g. "parent's surgery + this
  year's cash flow + your remaining vacation days" evaluated together)
- Major decisions: lists pros / cons / preconditions / kill criteria
- Doesn't decide for you — lists options and the cost of each

## Notes — this is the most sensitive directory

- Strongly recommend filesystem-level encryption for the whole archive
  (macOS FileVault / Linux LUKS)
- Do NOT sync to public clouds (OneDrive / Google Drive / Dropbox)
- Can redact identifying info ("M" / "father" / "housemate A" instead of real names)
- Don't write SSNs / national IDs / bank accounts here — use a password manager
- Divorce / legal dispute materials: consider a separately-encrypted file
